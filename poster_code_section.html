Pi camera captures a JPEG → OpenCV undistorts and detects an ArUco marker for real-world scale → tool silhouette is extracted and expanded for foam clearance → vtracer converts the mask to SVG → dimensions are patched to mm for the laser cutter. All parameters live in config.py.
